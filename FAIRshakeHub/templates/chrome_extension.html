How to install:

  1. Click "Download" button and unzip files.
  2. Open Google Chrome and go to "chrome://extensions".
  3. Enable Developer mode at the top right.
  4. Click "Load unpacked extension..." and choose the folder containing the downloaded files.
  5. Enable the FAIRshake extension in the Chrome extension list.
